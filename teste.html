quem somos

We're GasCash, a dynamic fintech specializing in fuel credit and fleet management, designed to simplify, secure, and organize refueling operations for drivers, businesses, and partner gas stations.

Our journey started when our diverse team—from technology to finance—recognized the challenges in fleet management and fuel cost control, and came together to innovate solutions on all sides of the industry.

Connecting users with a vast, trusted network of accredited stations, we deliver rapid credit, digital control, and exclusive benefits. From reducing bureaucracy and increasing control to granting freedom to daily operations, our tech-integrated solutions are modern, accessible, and designed for you.
